About Us

This website is built to reinvent your travel planning to be more efficient and convenient with the help of Juander Go. This will find you a range of options for travel itineraries based on the result of your test.

Juander Go is a website that generates travel itineraries and allows the tourist to know what kind of traveler they are through a plog test. The website allows the user to browse a variety of options for their future travel.

What type of Tourist are you?

Better Travel, Better Experience

Allocentrics

Travelers who are continually looking for new experience and destinations: they are adventurous by nature.

Midcentrics

Travelers who are not particularly adventures and there will be no self effort to visit bizarre places. But at the same time, these do not oppose experiments regarding touring in terms of activities or strange places. Hence they are very receptive to new experiences.

Psychocentrics

Travelers who prefer trips close to home and seek familiar environments, such as hotel chains with which they are comfortable when traveling.
